Our Story

We Build Websites
With Purpose

TinyBotics was founded with one mission: to make beautiful, professional websites accessible to every business in India — not just the big ones.

We believe that a great website should be within reach for a local restaurant, a small boutique, a freelancer, or a growing startup. Too many good businesses are invisible online because professional web design feels out of reach. We're here to change that.

Every website we build is handcrafted with attention to detail, performance, and your customer's experience. We don't use page builders or cheap templates — we write real code for real results.
