FAQ

Common Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Most websites are delivered within 7–14 business days depending on the complexity. E-commerce projects may take 14–21 days. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start.

Do you provide hosting?

+

We help you set up hosting on your preferred platform (GitHub Pages for free hosting, or Hostinger, GoDaddy for premium hosting). We guide you through the entire process.

Can I update the website myself?

+

Yes! We build easy-to-manage sites and provide a handover guide. If you need changes, we're also available for ongoing support at reasonable rates.

What do I need to provide?

+

Just share your business details, logo (if any), photos, and any content you want on the site. Don't have these? We can help with content writing and stock images too!

Do you offer revisions?

+

Absolutely. Every project includes up to 3 rounds of revisions to make sure you're completely happy with the final product.
